About Orgena Studio

Orgena Studio is an open-source game engine designed to empower developers and creators to build immersive experiences easily and efficiently. With a focus on extensibility, performance, and user-friendly tools, Orgena aims to be the ultimate choice for indie developers and studios alike.

The engine is actively maintained by a passionate community, continuously evolving to meet modern game development needs.
